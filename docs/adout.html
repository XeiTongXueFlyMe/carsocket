关于我们

成都市悦牛信息技术有限公司坐落于中国新一线城市成都市高新区，是一家致力于汽车一键启动、 手机控车等车联网产品研发、生产和销售的现代科技新型企业，具有雄厚的技术力量和先进的产品理念。 公司团队是一支经验丰富，锐意创新的研发团队，核心成员均来自智能汽车领域及IT界的资深企业，深耕汽车产品研发近10年。

Chengdu YueNiu Information Technology Co., Ltd. is located in Chengdu High-tech Zone, a new front-line city in China. It is a new modern technology enterprise dedicated to the research, development, production and sales of automobile networking products such as one-button start-up, mobile phone control and so on. It has strong technical strength and advanced product concept. The company's team is an experienced and innovative R&D team. The core members are all from senior enterprises in the field of intelligent automobile and IT. They have been engaged in R&D of automobile products for nearly 10 years.
